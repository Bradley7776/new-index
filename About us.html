About us

Welcome

Welcome to ROSS Training School. The school has been in operation for 21 years and has grown from a fledgling school with just 10 students, 23 teachers and 1 principal to a vibrant learning community that currently has more than 300 students from over 50 countries and territories, faculty and staff come from all around the world. world. The school is determined to become a leader in international education in Zhejiang Province.

Holding degrees from well-credentialed universities,western teachers come fully qualified to teach and represent many years of experience in their areas of teaching expertise. The local Chinese staff are also highly competent,most with English Language degrees, Chinese teaching qualifications and bilingual skills.
